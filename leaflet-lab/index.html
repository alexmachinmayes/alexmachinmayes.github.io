Declining US Cities

The 40 US cities with the highest percentage of population loss (2010-2017)


Proportional and dynamic city circle markers are utilized to show the range of percent population losses for all cities displayed within the map extent you choose. The map legend shows the maximum and minimum values and the mean of those values as a dynamic reference.
The slider control can be used to move through the year ranges (2011 through 2017) illustrating the cumulative population decreases in these cities since 2010.



*Data from US Census Bureau population estimates. Percentage population loss calculation: ((pop2010 - pop20XX) / pop2010) * 100
